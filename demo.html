SoundEffectManager Demo

All it's doing is this to get things ready:


window.sm = new SoundEffectManager();
sm.loadFile('taps.mp3', 'taps');
sm.loadFile('rocket.wav', 'rocket');

Then sm.play('rocket') to play it.
